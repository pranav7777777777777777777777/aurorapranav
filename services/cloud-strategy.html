Our Featured Services

CLOUD READINESS ASSESSMENT

Before moving your business to Cloud, it is essential to undertake the Cloud Readiness Assessment of your organization. It is a comprehensive process that studies organizational readiness, application discovery and application assessments. It provides your company a clarity of vision and a well laid path to successfully migrate all your operations to cloud infrastructure. Our experts utilize tested readiness assessment tools that put you in the right direction. As companies begin to increasingly adopt Cloud servers and services, it is going to yield greater benefits if it is implemented in the correct procedure. We conduct in-depth organizational analysis following which we devise prescriptive measures to make your business cloud-ready.

CLOUD STRATEGY

As a trusted cloud partner, we help you develop a strategic and tactical plan on how to get the best of the cloud servers for your business. We help you in detailing the strategy, in meticulous planning and implementation of the plan on your journey to cloud implementation. We take a comprehensive approach in examining both the current and future states of your business needs and the IT infrastructure while devising the strategy. Our focus is to go beyond the technical layer and understand your business case before crafting an overarching roadmap that encompasses all departments of your business.

CLOUD SECURITY

Whether you have migrated your business to cloud or strategizing to do so, it is necessary to enhance your data security while using cloud services. We help you assess cloud-based risks and security gaps associated with it and take necessary steps in protecting from the impending risk. The move to cloud should inspire further innovation, and being secure is a key aspect in this regard. Our security experts help you uncover any underlying risks in the applications or in the infrastructure setup or in the network security or in the instance configuration. Having a comprehensive security strategy will pave the way for an efficient implementation and usage of cloud server.

Business Centric Perspective

Customer Challenge

Cloud offers a banquet of options for companies to upscale their business. The domain of cloud is so vast that companies are often overwhelmed with which road to take. This partly arises due to a lack of proper planning before companies migrate to cloud. Creating a roadmap of cloud migration is necessary to extract the best of what cloud has to offer. Companies do not focus on the aspects of security, post-migration to cloud. Every enterprise must have a security strategy in place to robustly tackle issues arising in cloud usage. Cloud preparedness is of paramount importance for successful migration.

Our Solution

We develop an action driven Cloud strategy that strikes the perfect balance between business agility, innovation, security and service efficiency. We turn your business vision into a reality with the right guidance on every step of the way. We strive to craft a roadmap that is realistic and achievable. Our experts utilize state-of-the-art tools that help you with cloud preparedness on the technology and security fronts. We devise an efficient cloud strategy that takes into consideration the specific roadmaps for cloud adoption without sacrificing quality of service. Our strategic partnership helps you assess the current state of applications and recommend the most efficient options.

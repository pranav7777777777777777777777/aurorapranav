The Executive Team

Babu Ravi Kumar P

Founder, Aurora e-Labs - M.Tech-IISc, MBA-IIMA

With over 11 years of experience, Babu brings a strong understanding of how technology works and excels at translating strategies into actionable results. He leads Aurora e-Labs’ strategic direction, business development, process innovation, and ensures client satisfaction.

Before joining Aurora e-Labs, Babu held leadership roles as Executive Director at Vignan Group Schools, Senior Sales Manager at iDiscoveri Education, Lead Consultant at Cognizant, and Technical Leader at Tejas Networks. He holds an M.Tech from IISc Bengaluru and an MBA from IIM Ahmedabad. In his free time, Babu enjoys reading, blogging, and outdoor activities.

Ram Kotha

CEO, Aurora e-Labs - M.Sc (Information Systems), ISB-PCPM

Ram Kotha, the CEO of Aurora e-Labs, brings a strong client-focused approach to leadership, ensuring customer satisfaction remains at the core of the company's solutions.

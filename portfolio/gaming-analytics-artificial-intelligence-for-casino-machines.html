Client: Gaming Analytics.ai

Empowering Casino Operators with Artificial Intelligence

INDUSTRY: Information Technology

Services Used

Website Link

Gaming Analytics is a Silicon Valley based product company started by a team of Data Scientists and Senior Executives with more than 125 years of collective experience in casino gaming industry. The company has developed an innovative product for Casino Operators using latest technological power of AI in providing Machine level business Intelligence. Product development was driven with clear focus on delivering extraordinary customer experience, complete customization to the customer and machine driven analytical insights. Within no time, the product was recognized by Casino Journal as one of the top 3 innovative technology products for 2017 in the gaming industry. The innovation has also gained market support & investment from well-established leaders in gaming industry like “NRT Tech”. This is the first self-learning product based on Artificial Intelligence to help gaming operators in making good business decisions.

Scope of the work

Building a next generation product with the latest technologies require multiple highly qualified teams to come together and contribute their 2 cents. Having previously worked with Aurora on another product, Gaming team has engaged us in this product development with full cognizance on our capabilities and expertise. Three different teams were working on the product and we were expected to work along with them and do our part. We were all working with a single goal to provide compelling user experience and complete customization ability to the customer.

Aurora Work

From day one, it was very clear that the success of this project does not depend on our expertise in latest technologies alone but also on the ability of the teams to collaborate and work as one unit. Three different teams were working on this project from three locations and different time zones. We have used multiple collaboration platforms to ensure that there is complete clarity on the development efforts, people are collaborating to share their knowledge with each other and derive mileage from each other’s work. The offshore development team was responsible for implementing cutting edge features on the customer end with focus on performance & flexibility of customization. The approach of having distributed teams has paid off with a beautiful product with next generation scalability & customization features.

Mordor Intelligence is a global market research company which provides various services like Market research reports, Industry research reports, Custom Market research. It has demonstrated tremendous growth pattern by providing quality reports and utilizing technology at its best. Technology helps to scale operations, ensure quality and process transparency. The company is mainly into Finance & Investment, Energy & Power, Aerospace & Defense, Agriculture, Food & Beverage, Sensors & Electronics, Information & Communications Technology, Automotive & Machinery, Healthcare, Packaging, Chemicals & Materials, Freight & Logistics  to all over the world. To make its mark in the highly competitive market, the company has embraced technology & deployed scientific approach.

Scope of the work

Mordor approached us to revamp its already existing platform to a completely customized and configurable platform that can support its business growth. We had to study the existing application, its structure and SEO status & ensure that link score is not lost. This platform is to be built completely with custom features, enhancements and most importantly the system architecture to be fully supporting for expansion. It is a different kind of challenge to ensure that SEO score of the links is not disturbed and yet the application has to be revamped. 

Aurora work

We have carefully studied the existing application and the app structure to arrive at the system architecture. Our engineers have considered all corner cases and possible future applications before arriving at an architecture that has in reality supported the business in this growth stage. We have provided the best in class UI/UX to ensure that the most important information is always available to the customer who is visiting the website. The web application is very responsive and can be used on multiple browsers, devices, operating systems and it is providing services to millions of users and businesses. As we moved forward in contineous association, the scenarios were ever increasing & we have integrated lot of third party applications to build business intelligence data.

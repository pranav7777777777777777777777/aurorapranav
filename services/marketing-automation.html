Business Centric Perspective

Customer Challenge

As there are multiple communication channels coming up on a regular basis, it becomes difficult for companies to be present on all the channels & communicate with their target customers. Also, it becomes very difficult to aggregate the outcomes of marketing campaigns across all these platforms into a single data point for decision making. Companies have to invest in dedicated manpower & time for specific channels to ensure that unique message is going out to all the customers across the channels. Next, companies often generate volumes of content to market their product. However, distributing content across different channels and reaching out to the audience at the right time, still remains a big challenge.

Our Solution

We at Aurora understand the underlying need of Marketing Automation for companies. It is to narrow down their focus on individual customer and offer paramount customer service. We believe, for Automation platforms to be powerful, they need to be deeply integrated with your existing system. This helps you personalize the content across different platforms and provide an intent-driven customer experience. Our marketing automation service is built to streamline your customer acquisition efforts and maximize ROI for your business. With heavy influx of customer data nowadays, our solutions are robust and capable of absorbing the growing complexity.

About the Company

An organisation that stands for commitment with passion to drive results

Established in December of 2014 by IISc/IIM-A alumnus, Aurora e-Labs is a Design Company based out of HiTech City - Hyderabad. The core principle of the company is to provide solutions to real world business problem with design thinking.

Whether its a hard core technological problem or marketing challenges for existing product, we at Aurora E-Labs analyse and provide solutions by dividing the problem into multiple smaller pieces and resolving every piece with a design approach. We’ve served customers with diverse market presence, scale and operations (from early stage startups to large corporations). Deploying relevant technical architectures and marketing approaches, we’ve helped more than 45+ clients, operating in 3 countries with about 80,000+ monthly unique visitors .

We stretch ourselves to delight our customers by delivering cutting edge solutions with best in class user experience at the core of it.

Our creative in-house design team delivers the best User Interface (UI) and the best User Experience (UX) to provide pleasant, intuitive & engaging experience to your customers. Designing the best UI/UX every time & keeping it relevant to the client business is only possible through process driven approach.

  • We understand your business & your customer before going to canvas
  • Mockups are presented with typography, color schema and visual elements
  • Aesthetics, Functionality & User Experience are ensured with top priority
  • Clients get detailed view & approve their product UI right at the beginning

The efficiency of any system is defined by its process. All our actions are guided by process and driven by results. We follow Agile Methodology along with Just in Time (JIT) practice to manage efficient task execution with minimum possible delays. Test first approach and cross domain knowledge helps in better programming.

  • Our process begins right from the recruitment stage in identifying passion
  • Agile with JIT (Just in Time) helps to reduce blocks in development cycle
  • Test First Methodology brings the developer closer to testing scenarios
  • Learning Sessions are conducted to impart cross domain knowledge

We know the value of being on time and we invest more than 100% efforts to make sure you are on time. Client engagement model, internal development process, transparency and clear communication is the key for timely delivery. Attention to details is required at each step from requirement gathering to deployment.

  • Detailed project analysis by Business Analysts helps to give realistic timelines
  • Breaking the project into weekly deliverables is important for timely delivery
  • Mandatory Weekly Client Feedback always keeps a check on the expectation
  • Weekly Delivery Model is helping clients to experience incremental product

We understand how it feels if someone leaves you in the middle of an entrusted work. We value your association and will always strive to be at your support even after completing the development process. We prefer to be your technology partner by hand-holding in your technology decisions & enable growth.

  • Dedicated support after deploying the product into production servers
  • Our delivery process ensures that different time-zones does not matter
  • Contineously monitor the performance of the product for improvements
  • We are always a call away to resolve your issues and suggest next enhancements

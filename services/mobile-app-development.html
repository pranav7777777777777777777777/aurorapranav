Our Featured Services

iOS APP DEVELOPMENT

We develop high-performance iOS Mobile Apps that seamlessly integrate with the device core system. Developing a Mobile App that’s intuitive, robust and secure is key to winning customer engagement & confidence. Our strong technical team has expertise in iOS specific technologies & tools that help in developing sophisticated user-centric mobile apps. The team has built applications like Voice-enabled apps using Siri, Offline data applications using on-device storage, Customer payments using Apple Pay, IoT apps that can interact with sensors and others. All our apps are completely responsive & compatible across all Apple devices.

ANDROID APP DEVELOPMENT

We develop high-impact Android Apps, built for better user engagement. Our UI & UX teams always strive to deliver sleek and minimal designs leveraging the inherent Android OS capability. The user-centric design approach helps us solve the challenges of reaching out to the desired audience. Our team has rich development experience in native Android technologies & have delivered app features like offline apps with local storage, auto-synchronizing, REST API connections, geo-fencing apps, secure payment gateways. We have developed best-in-class android apps with varying complexities and helped many businesses in reaching out to their customers.

HYBRID APP DEVELOPMENT

Hybrid Apps make it easier for a business to launch its Minimum Viable Product at a much faster pace, reduced effort and yet achieve the performance of a native application. This innovative approach provides the best alternative to native apps. Our technical team is well versed with development tools like Cordova, IONIC, React Native and several others. We have delivered hybrid apps like Event Management, Asset Tracking that required features like data synchronization, geo-tagging of assets, app analytics. We focus on developing a Hybrid App that performs optimally across all platforms.

Business Centric Perspective

Customer Challenge

It has become mandatory for a business organization to be available 24/7 for servicing its customers. Mobile Apps have made this a possibility. Today, there are multiple technological alternatives to get a mobile app for business. In the midst of all this, it is very difficult for a business owner to decide the best suitable option. A wide variety of devices and their core systems make it all the more complicated to choose the correct platform. Also with exponential growth of data, Apps have to be carefully designed for security and privacy concerns.

Our Solution

At Aurora, we have a dedicated Mobile Application Development team with a wide range of skills in Native & Hybrid App Development. We handhold our clients in technology decision making by providing inputs to choose the right kind of platform. Innovative solutions are developed for the problem to deliver best-in-class UI/UX, high performance, better navigation & efficient device resource utilization. A User-focussed approach helps us build engaging Mobile Apps that leave a lasting impression on users. We understand how crucial the customer data is for companies and we take up Data Security and Privacy concerns as vital components of our development process.
